FAQ

How do I get started?

Poptart is not a very complicated bot to use. Just tag the bot or use .help to get started.

What can your bot all do?

Poptart has over 140 commands. Poptart delivers moderation commands with log, fun commands for random converstions, RPG commands to enjoy an RPG game and information commands to retrieve information on other discord users/servers.

Can I join your team?

I used DiscordGo and DBL language. I also made other bots with dicord.js and discord.py.
